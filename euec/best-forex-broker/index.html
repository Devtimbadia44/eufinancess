Finding the Most Popular Forex Broker for Beginners

1. Trading Instruments

It is good to diversify. That means investing in several markets at once. We provide a wide range of instruments and markets. They include CFDs on commodities, cryptocurrency, stocks, indices, and ETFs.

The best forex broker companies in the world, try their best to make sure that they have as many instruments as possible, for their clients.

For a client, having access to many instruments allows you to diversify. More often than not, many traders lose their investments entirely because they did not expand their investments. Having your funds distributed to different trades in various markets will allow you the freedom to have the potential of making more profits and mitigating the acute effects of misfortune.

2. A Variety of Accounts

The best forex broker offers several account types for you to choose from. Accounts have different trading styles. Some have higher protections, better leverages, and spreads and different instruments.

As a trader, you must pick the right account for you. The accounts are usually created in a way that allows the traders to choose one that works for their particular style of trading. Your entire strategy is hinged on the account type.

With some accounts, your style of trading may not bear any fruits. To understand them, let us briefly look at what they are and what they entail.

    • Micro/Mini Accounts- These types of accounts are suitable for traders with small amounts of capital. You can enter the market with a minimum deposit of $100 or less. The low entry barrier is a good thing, but once you are inside, you will be restricted to trading nano and micro-lots. It controls your risk levels and is perfect for beginners in trading
    • Standard Accounts- Also called Classic, Intermediate and other names, this type of account has a minimum deposit of between $100 and $500. You can trade mini lots with it. Some rare accounts can allow you to trade standard lots. They are a broker’s regular offering.
    • VIP/Professional Accounts- The name implies you need accreditation to enter, but that is not the case. They have a high minimum deposit of around $10,000 and allow you to trade standard lots. These accounts are usually ECN types. In Europe, the rules are a bit different and require documentation and credentials for you to get high leverages with these types of accounts.

Aside from these, we also have the demo accounts and the swap-free accounts.

3. Spreads

We recommend forex reviews to search for a broker with the lowest spread. However, the brokers might advertise a low spread and not have it. In most markets, the aim for most traders is to look for the lowest spreads because they allow you to trade cheaply. That is why the best forex brokers offer low spreads.

The trader wants to have a profitable strategy that will allow them to lower the transaction costs, as much as possible. Since the spread is the difference between the bid and asks prices, standard accounts will try to charge as much as they can without oppressing the trader too much.

Just like you, the broker is trying to make money. As a trader, unless you have an ECN account, you cannot just buy or sell a currency. The broker delivers quotes from both sides of the market. The difference between the two prices is profit, and some of it has to go to the broker.

4. Optional Leverage

We trade the forex market on leverage. Often, the best forex broker offers leverage in different levels. It is up to you to know which level is suitable for you. Leverage is borrowed capital to increase the potential returns.

Typically, the leverage in forex exceeds the invested capital several times. Leverage sizes are not fixed in all companies and depend on the trading conditions provided by specific brokers. How does one know which is the perfect leverage? The most popular leverage is 1:100.

However, there is a problem with leverage if it is too high. Yes, you have the potential to make so much money. Yet, you also have the potential to make huge losses that will leave you gasping for breath.

The best thing to do is not rush into using too much leverage. If you can avoid it entirely and go for margins, you will be safer. The point is, to do only what you know you can do. Leverage should be provided so that when you know you are ready to use it, you can access it quickly and make money.

5. Order Execution

Low spreads are useful when you start with a broker. However, having your orders executed at the asking price is even better. In the forex market, we have two types. They are; Instant Execution and Market Execution. The top forex brokers provide this.

The difference between them breaks down like this:

Instant Execution:

  • Execution takes 3-5 seconds
  • You could get requites
  • The execution is dependent on the market
  • The access to the market is through a dealer
  • The broker sets the price
  • There is no opportunity to open the order at a better price
  • The trading strategies, like hedging, scalping, news trading and the use of EAs is limited.

Market Execution:

  • Execution takes fractions of a second
  • There are no requotes
  • The execution is guaranteed
  • You have direct access to the market
  • The price is provided directly from the market
  • There are opportunities to place an order at a better price
  • There are no limits to strategies.

As you can see, there is no reason why you should not get to enjoy execution speeds that are high and have all the opportunities to place orders at a better price and trade with no limits to the strategies you can use.

6. Scalping Availability

The best fx broker does not have limits on trading styles. Scalping brokers do not set a minimum period to wait between the market price, the take-profit or the stop-loss. To make this more straightforward and easier to understand, scalping is a strategy that involves the skimming of small profits regularly by getting in and out of multiple positions quickly during a trading day.

In the forex exchange market, some of the brokers do not have provisions for that. For that reason, you should always check to see that this strategy is allowed before you sign up. There are several advantages for you when scalping is allowed.

Scalping is quite similar to day trading because you will open a position and then close it again during the current trading session, never carrying a position to the next session or overnight. In day trading, however, you may want to take a position once or twice or a little more than that per day.

Scalping has a frenetic feel to it because you will have to trade multiple times a day during a session. The average time of scalpers in a trade lasts about 5 to 30 minutes. Scalping requires the use of tick charts and one-minute charts.

As you will find out, you can make more money by using these strategies, after you are confident enough about how they work.
