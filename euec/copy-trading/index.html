What does that mean anyway? Well, to answer your question, we have to know what the copy trading is all about.

A Brief Explanation of Copy trading

We can describe copy trading as a process where you can automatically copy every operation executed by another trader, to get the same results in your account.

With copy trading, you are not handing over your funds to a fund manager. You will be handling them yourself. The only thing you will be getting is the information about a trader of your choice and what they do. Then, you just copy what they do.

So, we have:

  • The market
  • The broker
  • The trader (he who has the best trade decisions)
  • The investor (that would be you. Also called a follower)
